Eat Smart, Live Better 🍎

Good nutrition is the foundation of good health. Make every meal count with these smart choices:

🌿 Daily Healthy Habits

  • 🥦 Include green vegetables and seasonal fruits
  • 🍠 Choose whole grains like oats, brown rice, and quinoa
  • 🥜 Include healthy fats (avocados, nuts, seeds)
  • 💧 Drink 6–8 glasses of water daily
  • ⏱️ Eat on time and avoid skipping meals

🚫 Avoid Excess...

  • 🧂 Limit added salt and processed snacks
  • 🍭 Cut down on sugary drinks and candies
  • 🍟 Skip deep-fried or fast food frequently
  • 🍾 Minimize alcohol and caffeine
🍽️ Sample Balanced Meal
  • 🥗 Half plate of vegetables (raw or steamed)
  • 🍗 Quarter plate lean protein (chicken/fish/legumes)
  • 🍚 Quarter plate whole grains (rice/roti/oats)
  • 🥛 1 glass of water or low-fat milk
