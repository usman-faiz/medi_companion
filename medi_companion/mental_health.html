Your Mental Well-being Matters 💚

At Medi_Companion, we understand that mental health is just as important as physical health. It's okay to take a step back and prioritize your well-being. You are not alone.

🧠 Tips for a Healthy Mind

  • 🧘 Practice daily mindfulness or meditation (5–10 min)
  • 📞 Call or chat with a trusted friend or counselor
  • 🚶 Take a 20-minute walk in fresh air
  • 📖 Read something uplifting or educational
  • 🛌 Sleep well — aim for 7–9 hours nightly

🌐 Get Help — You're Not Alone

If you're struggling, consider reaching out to:

  • Pakistan Mental Health Helpline: 1166
  • BetterHelp: www.betterhelp.com
  • 7 Cups: www.7cups.com
